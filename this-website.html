Learning by Creating

In Fall 2017, I took a course on Constructionism where my semester-long project was to "build something". No guidelines, no training wheels. I had always wanted to learn front-end web development, so I decided to do it by designing my own website.

The website you're on is the fruit of that labor and a process that continues today. Interested in how I taught myself?
